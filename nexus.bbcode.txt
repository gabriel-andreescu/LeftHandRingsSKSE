[size=4][color=#00ffff]OVERVIEW[/color][/size]
Adds proper left-hand ring support, so you can wear a second ring.

The Bond of Matrimony can also use its own left-hand slot if enabled, which means you can wear up to three rings total.


[size=4][color=#00ffff]HOW IT WORKS[/color][/size]
When you assign a ring to the left hand, the mod makes a hidden duplicate of that same ring and equips the duplicate in a separate left-hand slot.

That copy keeps the important stuff from the original ring, like its model, enchantments, display name, and relevant script behavior. The only real difference is that the ring is shown on the left hand instead of the right.

This works best with rings that are set up like normal Skyrim rings. Most vanilla-style rings should be fine.

Most mod-added rings I could find worked fine too, but if a ring uses some weird custom equip system, unusual scripts, or a non-standard model setup, it may need testing.


[size=4][color=#00ffff]BALANCE[/color][/size]
By default, left-hand ring enchantments keep their full strength.

There is also an optional setting that can reduce the left-hand ring's enchantment strength while you are wearing an enchanted ring on the right hand.


[size=4][color=#00ffff]BOND OF MATRIMONY[/color][/size]
The Bond of Matrimony can be handled separately from normal rings.

If enabled, it uses its own left-hand slot and sits on the left ring finger. This is fully configurable.


[size=4][color=#00ffff]CONTROLS[/color][/size]
Rings now work like any other one-handed equip item.

Use [b]Equip[/b] for the right hand and [b]Left Equip[/b] for the left hand.


[size=4][color=#00ffff]CONFIGURATION[/color][/size]
You can edit the INI directly:

[quote]MCM\Config\LeftHandRingsSKSE\settings.ini[/quote]

Or use the optional MCM addon to change the same settings in-game.

[list]
[*]Left ring slot. Default: slot 58.[/*]
[*]Separate Bond of Matrimony slot. Default: enabled.[/*]
[*]Bond of Matrimony slot. Default: slot 59.[/*]
[*]Left ring enchantment strength while an enchanted right-hand ring is worn. Default: 100%.[/*]
[/list]

The slot menus include the commonly used mod body slots from 44 to 61.

If another mod uses the same slot, choose a different one.
